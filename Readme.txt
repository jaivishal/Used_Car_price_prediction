Used Car Price Prediction

This project is an approach to used car price prediction that focuses on feature selection and ConvolutionalBi-Directional_Gated_Recurrent_Unit which can assist in bringing into focus the signifance of newly emerging features. we have chosen this approach to emphasis the importance of newly emerging features and their impact on the process of predicting the price of used cars.
